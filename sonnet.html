Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlowSaaS.

How does the 14-day trial work? ⌄

Our 14-day trial gives you full access to all features of your selected plan. No credit card required, and you can cancel anytime. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or your account will automatically downgrade to our free plan with limited features.

Can I change plans later? ⌄

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the next billing cycle.

Is my data secure? ⌄

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. Our platform is hosted on secure servers with regular backups, and we comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions? ⌄

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified nonprofits, educational institutions, and startups. Please contact our sales team for more information.

Can I integrate FlowSaaS with other tools? ⌄

Yes, FlowSaaS integrates with popular tools like Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zapier, and many others. We also offer an API for custom integrations in our Professional and Enterprise plans.

What kind of support do you offer? ⌄

We offer email support for all plans, with priority support for Professional plans and 24/7 dedicated support for Enterprise customers. Our knowledge base and community forum are available to all users.
